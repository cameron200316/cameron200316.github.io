# Collins Aerospace
## Embedded Software Engineering Internship
Details:
* Location: Burnsville, MN
* Duration: May 2025 to Present

Through this role, I worked on embedded software for the Integrated Air Data Systems (IADS) team. During my tenure, I made significant contributions by developing test software to identify and resolve issues with test stations. This gave me exposure to various test software, including NI-Max and Promira. I created GUIs and DLLs in C# using Visual Studio to interact with test hardware.   

I have continued in this role part-time during the school year and will return to it full-time after graduation.

# Iowa State University
## Teaching Assistant 
Details:
* Location: Ames, IA
* Duration: August 2023 to May 2024

I was a teaching assistant for the introductory digital logic class at Iowa State (CPRE 281). This class focused on core concepts, including logic gates, 2's complement, and K-maps. As a TA, I gave a one-hour recitation at the beginning of the lab to help students with concepts learned in the lecture. Then, after the recitation, I would help students work through the lab.
